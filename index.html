About Me

I am a Research Scientist in Database and Storage Lab at Alibaba DAMO Academy. Prior to joining Alibaba, I was a Research Fellow in DB System Group at National University of Singapore. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from National University of Singapore, and my B.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, China.

Our Database and Storage Lab conducts research on latest challenges for database and storage systems in the cloud era. To support both online services within Alibaba Group and enterprise customers on Alibaba Cloud, we are working towards building reliable, secure, intelligent, performant and globally replicated database systems and services.

Research Interests

I am interested in solving research problems and building practical systems for real-world products:

  • Cloud-Native Database Architectures and Kernels
  • Encrypted, Verifiable and Trustworthy Databases
  • Data Storage and Indexing
  • Data Analytics Systems
  • Blockchain Systems
